Welcome Statement

Welcome to my electronic portfolio. In my electronic portfolio site are samples of writing and academic accomplishment in which reflects my aspirations of becoming a elementary school teacher and community leader. Graduating from San Francisco State with a major in Liberal Studies with an emphasis in Elementary Teaching and a minors in Asian American Studies and Japanese. 

I grew up in a world where Japanese culture and American culture were intertwined, as one modern phenomenon of great understanding. Where I spoke Japanese-English, learn of teachings of Buddhist and Christian philosophy, I lived in communities of San Francisco Bay Area and Matsudo-shi, Chiba and Tokyo. Once I left the playground years, I began to grasp a perspective of my own with a relaization of why I struggled through academia throughout my academic career. At a young age I didn't receive the proper nourishment of understanding languages.

I began to recognize all the room for improvements within the environment we all live in. I am in pursuit of following the steps of the peacemakers before me, and reveal the hushed voices of under-represented people to the rest of the world.

I am mindful of respect, space, and politeness to all beings. Constantly I reflect and alerter my perspectives into ways I can gain in self and understanding of others. Thus, my major Liberal Studies is a collection of interdisciplinary studies in which I can study and grasp multiple concepts in all areas of academia.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about my passions in development here at San Francisco State University.

Six word memoir: Passion, Advocacy, Determination, to bettering society
